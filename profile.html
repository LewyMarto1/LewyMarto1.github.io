Online Test Results

I partook in three tests to get a self-evaluation of what traits I possess, the tests include a Myers-Briggs Test, a Learning Style Test and a Big Five Personality Test. The results from the selected tests told me a few things about myself.

Myers-Briggs Test

I got an ENTJ result which means that I have an Extraverted, Intuitive, Thinking and Judging personality, this means that I have higher level of charisma and confidence and have leadership qualities. I will use my drive and determination to achieve which ever goal is set for me.

Learning Styles Test

I got a result of 45% as a visual learner, 35% tactile and 20% Auditory, this means that I am predominately a visual learner, I prefer to drop my books and learn while watching. This result makes a lot of sense to me, as I have always thought of myself as a visual learner.

Big Five Personality Test

I got a results telling me that I am low range in my openness to exposure telling me that I am down to earth and practical, I am middle range in conscientiousness which means that I am not the most productive person but still not disorganised, I have a middle range in Extraversion which means there are times where I enjoy being by myself and times where I enjoy being with others, I have a low range in Agreeableness meaning I am competitive and hard headed and I have a very low range in Natural reactions meaning I am very relaxed most of the time.
